ClickFix ~ Tame your mouse

A Problem

The average lifespan of a moderately priced computer mouse is too short. Although advances in repair techniques allow computer mice to recover and live onwards, these repairs require equipment, knowledge and the acceptance of some risk. It's also entirely possible that a hardware-based repair will fail to resolve the issue. If you decide to chase a software solution, you will likely not find something that works properly, or at all - except in one case:

A Solution

ClickFix is, to my knowledge, the only working software solution for click-happy mice. If your mouse exhibits multiple clicks when a button is only pressed once, it may have a problem that ClickFix can solve. It is trivial to set up ClickFix - many users set-and-forget in just under 1 minute. For more information about the inner workings of ClickFix, have a look at the Github repository.

Get Started

To get going with ClickFix, hit "Download" below. This will take you to the releases page, where you can choose the latest executable. After all that, it's a simple matter of putting it in a folder - and then running the executable.
